    • The Usual Suspects

    Character number 4: Terry Jeffords (Terry Crews)

    Terry is the 99th precinct's main Sargent, who may look tough on the outside, but is really a big old softie on the inside, who became a cop to protect his family from danger and discrimination all while protecting himself from the hijinks of the precinct.

    Character 5: Gina Linetti (Chelsea Peretti)

    Linetti isn't really a detective in the precinct, but rather she is the Civillian office manafer and somewhat Secretary to Captain Holt in the Precinct, where she may be tasked with cleaning up everyone else's mess, while also somehow getting involved in everyone else's business

    Character 6: Rosa Diaz (Stephanie Beatriz)

    Rosa is the detective of the 99th precinct who kicks butt first and ask questions later. She has been with the precinct for as long as Peralta has and yet even after that lenght of time, none of the other officers don't know everything about her past or what else what she will do next.

    Character 7: Charles Boyle (Joe Lo Truglio)

    Boyle is one of the most hardest working officers in the 99th precinct who always come up with crazy ideas, but who also may say some awkward comments. While is a familt man with his adopted son Nikolaj and his wife, he also has an idol in the form of his best friend and partner Jake Peralta.
