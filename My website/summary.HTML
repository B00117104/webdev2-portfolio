    So you want a basic summary of what this tv series is all about and why you should watch it, well you have come to the right place because I will give you the basic understandings of this show


    So Brooklyn Nine-Nine is an American Comedy cop drama that follows the work and most of the time, hijinks and mishaps of the fictional 99th precinct of the NYPD in Brooklyn. It sees the daily jobs of seven unique characters including carefree yet talented cop Jake Peralta, capt. Ray Holt, hardworking yet very organised Amy Santiago, the charasmatic Charles Boyle, Sgt. Terry Jeffords, a devoted family man, the sexy yet intimidating Rosa Diaz and the civilian office manager Gina Linetti. Together they all get involved in crazy situations, madcap heists and just daily life overall.
