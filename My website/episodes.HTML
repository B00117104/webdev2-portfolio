    Over the span of Five seasons there have been a total release of 112 episodes with much more to come in newer seasons. But while there is time, let's give a brief run down summary of what you can expect from each of the seasons released so far

    Season 1

    We are introduced to the 99th precinct and it's new captain Raymond Holt who clashes with Peralta, due to his immaturity. Later in the season, the precinct's annual Halloween heist is established, Boyle get's shot in the butt protecting Detective Diaz, they learn about Holt's home life and past and Peralta begins to develop fellings for fellow detective Santiago after winning a year long bet.


    Season 2

    Jake returns to the precinct after going undercover at the end of season 1 to take down the Mafia. He then begins a relationship with defense lawyer Sophia Perez, but ends because of their different jobs. Holt begins a battle of wits with Deupty Commissioner Wunth that ends with Wunth making Holt head of NYPD public affairs. Gina and Boyle end up in a lenghty relationship, until it is found out and ends and culminates with Charle's dad and Gina's mom getting married. Rosa begins a relationship with Holt's nephew Marcus and a relationship begins to blossom between Jake and Amy during an undercover mission.

    Season 3

    the 99 gets a new captain for a day before he dies of a heart attack after seeing Jake and Amy kissing, then the Vulture assumes command until Holt retakes it with help from Jake after catching a serial killer. Boyle begins a relationship with an Art Curator. Terry's wife gives birth to their third daughter. Rosa relationship with Marcus ends but begins a new one with detective Adrian Pimento, who's been undercover for 12 years. They get engaged before Pimento goes into hiding from a crime lord known as Figgis who wants him dead. Amy goes undercover in a women's prison to figure out the informant in the FBI trying to kill Pimento to discover it is Holt's former partner Bob Annderson. they stop him and take down Figgis's empire, before calling Peralta and threatening his and Holt's lives forcing them to go into witness protection in Florida.

    Season 4

    Peralta and Holt stay in witness protection in Florida until the 99 help them to take down Figgis. Because of this the squad is subsequently punished and forced to do the night shift, but get back to day shift when Substitute captain C.J Stently is transferred from active duty for his incompetence. Charles and his wife adopt a son named Nikolaj. Adrian return and resumes his romance with Rosa, but call off their wedding and take their time with their relationship.


    Jake and Amy move in together after Amy wins a bet for catching the most escaped criminals. Gina gets hit by a bus and after she recovers she becomes pregnant with a child with Boyle's cousin, Milton. She also saves the precinct from being shut down. Terry becomes a victim of Racial Profiling and Peralta and Rosa compete for a spot on the task force of their idol Lt.Hawkins, but discover that she is a dirty cop and they are framed for Bank Robbery and sentenced to 15 years in prison.

    Season 5

    Jake and Rosa adjust to life in prison before the 99 take down Hawkins when Holt makes a deal with Mobster Seamus Murphy. When they return, Peralta relises he isn't ready to be back out on the field and Rosa breaks up with Pimento. During the Halloween Heist, Jake proposes to Amy and become engaged. On the trip to Los Angeles for the funeral of the precinct's previous captain, Holt learns he is in the running for the position of the new police Comissioner and the squad help him to deal with Murphy. Rosa comes out as Bisexual. Gina returns to the precinct after her maturnity leave. Though the squad find a loophole that allows Holt to keep his deal with Murphy without jepordizing his moral code, Murphy finds out and then threaten the life of Holt's husband, Kevin.


    Kevin is forced to stay in a safehouse with Peralta for two months to avoid detection from Murphy. After the 99 locate his hideout, Kevin takes down Murphy and him and his men are apprehended. Charles attempts to run his own food business. Amy passes her sargent's exam, and after their wedding plans fall apart from a bomb threat, Jake and Amy get married outside of the precinct. The season ends at the after-party of their wedding where the squad is unsure whether Holt won the comissioner's title or not.
