    • The Usual Suspects


    In Brooklyn Nine-Nine there are 7 distinct characters that each have their own unique character traits that help them stand out on their own

    Character 1: Jake Peralta (Andy Samburg)

    Jake Peralta is the character that lives and breathes the life of an NYPD officer. He may be kinda crazy and carefree coming up with different personas while undercover and thinking every case is his own version of Die Hard, he also takes the job seriously, especially when he is trying to impress capt. Ray Holt.

    Character 2: Captain Ray Holt (Andre Braugher)

    Capt. Raymond Holt is the man of many expressions from just one look on his face. He is the new captain of the 99th precinct who helps get the precinct back on top. He has been working as a police officer since the late 70s/ 80s but it has been difficult to certain extents due to the fact that he is a gay, black man, yet he has found the way through to become the captain he is today

    Character 3: Amy Santiago (Melissa Fumero)

    Amy Santiago is the detective that plays it by the book, but can also get down and dirty whenever the time comes for that. Whenever she is not trying to outperform her co-worker/love interest Peralta, she is there to try to keep herself in Holt's good books and is always there to help out when things get toit
